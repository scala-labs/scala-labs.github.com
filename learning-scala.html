The Big Learning Scala List

  • The official Scala site, start at the Learning Scala trail or take a tour of Scala.
  • First steps to Scala by Bill Venners, Martin Odersky, and Lex Spoon.
  • The famous "staircase" Scala book. Some sample chapters are available in PDF format.
  • The O'Reilly Scala book, by Dean Wampler and Alex Payne. The entire contents are online !
  • An alternative online tour of Scala
  • Try out Scala programming in your web browser.
  • Typesafe/Akka, the first offical enterprise stack offered by the creator's of Scala.
  • Lift, a web application framework for Scala.
  • Parsing XML with Scala

If you want to talk with your fellow Scala programmers, you can join the Scala IRC channel: irc://irc.freenode.net/scala
